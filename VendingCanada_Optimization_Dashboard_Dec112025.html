Executive Strategy & Intent Isolation

Over the last two days, we have executed a significant overhaul of the Vending Canada Google Ads account. The primary focus was moving from broad targeting to a high-granularity Single Keyword Ad Group (SKAG) structure. This shift allows us to isolate Purchase Intent (Sales) from Rental/Service Intent (Leads), ensuring that budget is spent efficiently and ad copy matches the user's exact search. We also implemented a robust UTM Tracking System to provide full visibility into which specific ads and keywords are driving revenue, fixing previous blind spots in data reporting.

Data-Driven Optimization Insights

Analysis of last 30 days' performance data revealing the need for SKAG restructuring.

üîç Keyword Analysis Findings

  • Broad Keywords Dominated: 70+ broad match keywords in "Search: Vending Machine" campaign generated 345 impressions but only 21 conversions (6.1% rate).
  • Mixed Intent Issues: Terms like "vending machine" triggered ads for both buyers and renters, wasting budget on irrelevant clicks.
  • Low Conversion Efficiency: Average CPL of $19.37 across broad keywords, with many terms showing 0 conversions despite impressions.
  • SKAG Readiness: New exact match SKAGs (e.g., [buy vending machine]) have 0 impressions yet, indicating fresh start for precise targeting.

üìä Search Term Behavior

  • Purchase Intent Terms: "buy vending machine" (16 interactions), "vending machines for sale" (40 interactions) showed strong commercial signals.
  • Service Intent Terms: "vending machine rental" (4 interactions), "coffee vending service" (2 interactions, 1 conversion) indicated lead generation potential.
  • Competitor Opportunity: "vending 4 less" achieved 1 conversion at $2.00 CPL, highlighting profitable conquesting potential.
  • Geographic Targeting: Terms like "vending machines canada" (16 interactions, 3 conversions) confirmed national demand.

üé® Asset Performance Insights

  • Top Performing Sitelink: "Used Vending Machines" (210 interactions, 9 conversions) proved value proposition effectiveness.
  • Logo Impact: Campaign logo generated 162 impressions with 10.5% interaction rate, enhancing brand trust.
  • Underperforming Assets: Several images and sitelinks had low CTR (1.3%), indicating need for optimization.
  • New Assets Ready: Recently added sitelinks for service intent have 0 impressions, awaiting SKAG activation.

üöÄ Why SKAG Restructuring?

  • Intent Isolation: Broad matching caused cross-contamination between purchase and service queries.
  • Cost Efficiency: Mixed intent led to higher CPL; SKAGs ensure budget targets exact buyer stages.
  • Quality Score Boost: Exact match keywords with tailored ads improve relevance and lower CPC.
  • Scalable Growth: Granular structure allows precise bidding and negative keywords for each intent.
