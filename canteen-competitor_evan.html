5. SEO / Marketing Insights

Local & Benefit Combination

Users often combine location + benefit in searches (e.g., “healthy vending Toronto”).

Competitive Landscape

Competitors are strong in healthy vending, micro-markets, and smart machines.

Primary Opportunity

Emphasize coast-to-coast coverage, robust corporate programs, and tech-enabled inventory systems.

PPC targeting should strategically include brand, service, healthy, and local intent ad groups.
