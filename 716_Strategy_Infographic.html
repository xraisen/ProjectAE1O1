Google Ads Blueprint ($800/mo)

With a targeted budget of $800, efficiency is paramount. We focus 75% of spend on "Bottom of Funnel" (High Intent) searches. We ignore broad generic terms to avoid waste.

Monthly Budget Allocation

01

Phrase Match Precision

Use "Furnished Apartment Buffalo" instead of Apartment Buffalo. This ensures we only pay for users specifically looking for our product type.

02

Negative Firewall

Aggressively block terms like "cheap", "studio", "unfurnished", and "1 night". This protects the budget from low-quality seekers.

03

Location Radius

Target people physically IN Buffalo + people searching FOR Buffalo from key feeder cities (NYC, Toronto).

04

Call Extensions

Enable "Click to Call" on mobile ads. Corporate bookers often prefer to call directly to verify availability immediately.
